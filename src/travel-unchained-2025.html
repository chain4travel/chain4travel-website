The 4th Travel Un'chained: A Recap

Travel Un'chained 2025 brought together handpicked executives, developers, and innovators from the travel and blockchain industries for three days of groundbreaking discussions and networking.

Set on the beautiful island of Crete, this edition delivered new insights, powerful connections, and the same game-changing energy that defines our community.

Where It Happened

Travel Un'chained 2025 took place at the 5-star Knossos Royal Resort, located directly on the northern coast of Crete. The world-class hospitality and Mediterranean luxury provided the perfect setting for meaningful connections, collaboration, and inspiration.
