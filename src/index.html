Today's travel industry

In today's travel industry, the innovation pace is decelerated; the development of new products and the technical improvement of existing ones are much slower than they could be, occasionally leading to standards and exchange protocols over 40 years old and, therefore, desperately needing replacement.

Tomorrow's travel industry

Blockchain technology has established itself in various industries in recent years, leading to massive efficiency and effectiveness gains. The travel industry is next. By moving processes to the Camino Network, every company in the industry can count on faster, cheaper, and future-proof strategies and highly customizable technology.

A new generation of internet is emerging

Web1

We aim to transform the travel industry from a fragmented and outdated industry environment into a unified and efficient global shared network. We believe this can only happen if we start working collaboratively. We offer our advice and guidance for taking the first steps with web3 in travel.

Web2

Chain4Travel is the initiator of Camino, the web3 travel ecosystem, and provides the KYC (Know-Your-Customer) process as well as the application suite for the Camino mainnet to ensure a fast, stable and secure environment for all travel enthusiasts while taking advantage of all blockchain-associated features.

Web3

Camino is a public & permissioned consortium blockchain specifically designed to cater to the needs of the travel industry and is operated and governed by key market participants. It provides unparalleled opportunities and tools for digital innovation and transformation for every player in the industry.

Web3 technology for digital innovation in the travel industry

The potentials for web3 innovation in the travel industry are huge. Looking at the pace of wallet integration, and digital identity, the next generation of travelers is emerging fast, and the industry is looking at vast transformations. Now is the time to explore web3 opportunities. We at Chain4Travel, help explore web3 opportunities and achieve digital innovation for your business.
