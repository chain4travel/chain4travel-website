Find more about us and how we innovate the travel industry with web3 technology

Advising first-movers in travel

We aim to transform the travel industry from a fragmented and outdated industry environment into a unified and efficient global shared network. We believe this can only happen if we start working collaboratively. We offer our advice and guidance for taking the first steps with web3 in travel.

Facilitating web3 technology

Chain4Travel is the initiator of Camino, the web3 travel ecosystem, and provides the KYC (Know-Your-Customer) process as well as the application suite for the Camino mainnet to ensure a fast, stable and secure environment for all travel enthusiasts while taking advantage of all blockchain-associated features.

Building a new operating system

Camino is a public & permissioned consortium blockchain specifically designed to cater to the needs of the travel industry and is operated and governed by key market participants. It provides unparalleled opportunities and tools for digital innovation and transformation for every player in the industry.

Facilitating the web3 travel ecosystem

Back in 2021, we initiated Camino, the web3 travel ecosystem. We built the features and entire infrastructure. However, we don't own the network, and all IP rights are given to the Camino Network Foundation. In the future, we will continue to serve as specialists in web3 technology and experts in the travel industry, bringing valuable insights and development efforts to Camino's advancement.

An infrastructure that is jointly governed by travel companies with equal voting power. A blockchain as unique as the industry itself and a "safe space" in the web3 environment. This is exactly what Camino is.

Camino Network, the web3 travel ecosystem

Camino is a powerful, highly scalable network for the travel industry of tomorrow. It is based on the robust and versatile Avalanche technology to ensure the highest security and low, predictable transaction costs. Companies in the travel industry can easily create applications in the Camino Network as all common development tools are supported out of the box.

Through Camino Network, all companies in the travel industry - no matter how large or small - are given the opportunity to future-proof their processes and benefit directly from the technological advances in the travel industry. The ease of use, the reliability, and the team of experienced industry professionals offer endless possibilities to integrate blockchain technology into your company structure already today.
