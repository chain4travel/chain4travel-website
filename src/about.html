However, we don't own the network, and all IP rights are given to the Camino Network Foundation. A global shared travel infrastructure

The travel industry is one of the largest in the world. It is time for it to have a unique global shared infrastructure - customized exactly as the travel industry needs and wants it. An infrastructure that is jointly governed by travel companies with equal voting power. A blockchain as unique as the industry itself and a "safe space" in the web3 environment. This is exactly what Camino is.

Camino Network, the web3 travel ecosystem

Camino is a powerful, highly scalable network for the travel industry of tomorrow. It is based on the robust and versatile Avalanche technology to ensure the highest security and low, predictable transaction costs. Companies in the travel industry can easily create applications in the Camino Network as all common development tools are supported out of the box.

Through Camino Network, all companies in the travel industry - no matter how large or small - are given the opportunity to future-proof their processes and benefit directly from the technological advances in the travel industry. The ease of use, the reliability, and the team of experienced industry professionals offer endless possibilities to integrate blockchain technology into your company structure already today.
