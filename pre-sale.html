What is vesting and locking?

Token vesting is a way to ensure the safety and confidence of early investors in new DeFi projects. The existing token vesting process involves the locking of tokens for a set period of time. During that time all bought tokens will be visible in the investorâ€™s wallet, however, they are not accessable (=locked).

  • 1 CAM = 0,10 CHF for 3 years (unlocking start after 2,5years, runs for 52 weeks)
  • 1 CAM = 0,08 CHF for 5 years (unlocking start after 4,5years, runs for 52 weeks)
  • 1 CAM = 0,15 CHF for 2 years (unlocking start after 1,5years, runs for 52 weeks)

The vesting mechanism foresees that 0,5 years prior to the ending of the official lock up period, a 52 week unlocking period starts. Every week, 1/52 of the total pre-sale token amount will be unlocked until after 0,5 years after the set date, all pre-sale tokens will be fully unlocked. The 52 weeks unlocking period ensures the chosen locking period complies on average with the actual years of token locking.
