The Camino blockchain

The Camino blockchain is a high-performance, highly scalable platform for the travel industry that supports the creation of sophisticated smart contracts. Camino is a consortium blockchain developed by Chain4Travel AG and operated and governed by key market participants of the tourism industry. Camino combines permissioned and permissionless blockchain solutions to solve the blockchain trilemma in favor of scalability while still being open to new participants, thus, becoming a semi- permissioned blockchain.
