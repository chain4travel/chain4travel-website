Camino Network

The Camino Network is a powerful, highly scalable platform for the travel industry of tomorrow. It is based on the robust and versatile Avalanche technology to ensure highest security and low, predictable transaction costs. This is also supported by the use of Proof-of-Stake & Authority (PoSA) as a validation method, the appointment of key market participants of the international travel industry as validators and an internal KYC processor. Companies in the travel industry can easily create applications on the Camino Network as all common development tools (e.g. Remix, Solidity, Viper, Truffle, and more) are supported out of the box. The applications take the form of backend software that is called by frontend applications (dApps).

The Camino Network gives all companies in the travel industry - no matter how large or small - the opportunity to future-proof their business processes and benefit directly from the technological advances in the travel industry. The ease of use, the reliability as well as the team of experienced industry professionals offer endless possibilities to integrate the blockchain technology into your company structure already today.
