Camino Network

The Camino Platform is a powerful, highly scalable platform for the travel industry of tomorrow. It is based on the robust and versatile Avalanche technology to ensure highest security and low, predictable transaction costs. This is also supported by the use of Proof-of-Stake & Authority (PoSA) as a validation method, the appointment of key market participants of the international travel industry as validators and an internal KYC processor. Companies in the tourism industry can easily create applications on the Camino platform as all common development tools (e.g. Remix, Solidity, Viper, Truffle, and more) are supported out of the box. The applications take the form of backend software that is called by frontend applications (dApps).

Through the Camino Chain, all companies in the travel industry - no matter how large or small - are given the opportunity to future- proof their own processes and benefit directly from the technological advances in the tourism industry. The ease of use, the reliability as well as the team of experienced industry professionals offer endless possibilities to integrate the blockchain technology into your company structure already today.

After actively participating in the travel industry for more than 25 years, I am highly impressed and excited about the potential in the current shift to blockchain technology. By giving the industry members easily accessible and customizable blockchain solutions, they will be at the forefront of this revolution.
