About us

Trading travel products has never been more accessible.

Camino is a blockchain-powered B2B(2C) ecosystem powered by the Camino Token, developed by Chain4Travel.

Camino eliminates the agreement phase’s effort before undertaking business while keeping risk low. With low transaction costs, Camino decreases blockchain finality to seconds.

Camino offers a versatile platform to map and expand tourism’s current business models and create new touristic products with these features.

The Camino Blockchain is a Decentralized Autonomous Organization operated, maintained, and governed by the travel industry.
